## Discovery without the noise

Aeon turns a large manhwa catalogue into focused feeds that are easy to browse. Move from widely loved titles to deep cuts, recent releases, completed stories, rising favourites, and hand-built collections without losing your place.

- **Swipe through focused feeds.** Home follows the order of your visible segments and remembers where you left each feed.
- **Build dynamic LIST feeds.** Create rule-based feeds with tags, dates, status, chapters, audience ranges, and layered sorting.
- **Curate MY LIST.** Collect titles from Search or any logic feed, arrange them manually, or apply automatic filters and sorting without changing membership.
- **Organize with segments.** Reorder, collapse, rename, export, and hide whole groups from Home while keeping their feeds available in the library.
- **See engagement in context.** Fan Rank highlights titles whose readers engage unusually strongly, not simply those with the largest audience.
- **Search naturally.** Find titles through primary names, aliases, word fragments in any order, and creator names. Search inside an individual feed without losing its original ranks.
- **Inspect each title.** See its synopsis, creators, publication status, chapters, tags, audience signals, and available source links.
- **Move collections between devices.** Share an individual feed by link or JSON, import feeds and segments without replacing the existing library, or export a full JSON backup.

## Two ways to build your Home

**LIST** is for living feeds. Choose the rules once, then let Aeon keep the results current as the catalogue changes.

**MY LIST** is for fixed collections. Add exactly the titles you want, keep a personal order or sort them automatically, and use filters without removing anything from the collection itself.

Both libraries use the same colourful segments. Their order and visibility decide what appears on Home, while hidden segments remain safely available in Feeds.

## Personal, without an account

Aeon does not require a sign-up. Your feeds, MY LIST collections, segments, display choices, and last app position stay with your browser. Sensitive catalogue areas remain hidden unless you choose to reveal them in Settings. A JSON backup lets you move or restore the complete setup when needed.

You can also add Aeon to your phone's Home screen for a focused, app-like reading-discovery experience.

## Catalogue and credits

[MangaBaka](https://mangabaka.dev/) supplies the main catalogue, covers, publication details, links, and tag hierarchy. [AniList](https://anilist.co/) supplies audience statistics for mapped titles. When available, Aeon also links to [MangaUpdates](https://www.mangaupdates.com/) and [Anime-Planet](https://www.anime-planet.com/) for further context.

Aeon is an independent discovery project and is not affiliated with these services or the publishers and creators represented in the catalogue.
